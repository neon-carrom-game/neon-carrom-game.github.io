Summary: Neon Carrom is committed to protecting your privacy. This application collects no personal information, requests no device permissions, and operates with minimal data collection limited solely to anonymous analytics provided by Google Analytics.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Personal Information

Neon Carrom does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details to use the application.

1.2 Device Permissions

Neon Carrom does not request or require any device permissions. The application does not access your:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Photo gallery or media files
  • Device storage
  • Phone state or identity

1.3 Analytics Data

The only data collection performed by Neon Carrom is through Google Analytics, which collects anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how users interact with the application and improve the gaming experience. The information collected by Google Analytics may include:

  • General device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Application usage patterns (screens visited, session duration)
  • Anonymous identifiers generated by Google Analytics
  • Approximate geographic location (country or region level only)

This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized manner and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous analytics data collected through Google Analytics is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Understanding application performance and user engagement
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Improving game features and user experience
  • Making informed decisions about future updates and enhancements

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since Neon Carrom does not collect any personal information beyond anonymous analytics data, there is no personal data to share or disclose. The analytics data collected by Google Analytics is subject to Google's privacy policy and is not shared with any third parties by us.

4. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your experience with Neon Carrom. Given that no personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted by the application, there are no security risks associated with personal data breaches. The anonymous analytics data is handled securely by Google Analytics in accordance with their security practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Carrom does not knowingly collect any information from children or individuals of any age. The application is designed to be safe for users of all ages, and no personal data collection mechanisms are implemented.
